How to Calculate Roofing Materials

Estimating roofing materials accurately prevents costly over-ordering or frustrating shortages mid-project. Our calculator accounts for roof pitch, number of sides, and a wastage buffer.

Don't Forget Underlayment

Roofing felt is installed beneath shingles as a moisture barrier. Standard 15 lb felt rolls cover approximately 400 square feet per roll.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate how many shingles I need?
Divide your total roof area by 100 to get roofing squares. Each square needs 3 bundles of standard shingles. Add 10–15% for waste from cutting around edges and valleys.
What is a roofing square?
A roofing square equals exactly 100 square feet of roof surface area. Three standard bundles of asphalt shingles cover one roofing square.
How much does a new roof cost?
Material costs for asphalt shingles average $1.50–$3.00 per square foot. Labour adds another $1.50–$3.00. Total installed cost typically ranges $5–$8.50 per square foot.
How much wastage should I add for roofing?
Add 10% for simple gable roofs. Add 15% for roofs with hips, valleys or skylights. Add up to 20% for complex roofs with multiple angles and dormers.
