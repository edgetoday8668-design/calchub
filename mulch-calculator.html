How Much Mulch Do I Need?

Mulch retains moisture, suppresses weeds, and regulates soil temperature. Getting the right amount prevents waste and ensures proper coverage.

Recommended Mulch Depth

PurposeDepthNotes

Standard garden bed3 inchesBest balance

Bulk vs Bagged Mulch

For areas under 2 cubic yards, bagged mulch is convenient. For larger projects, bulk mulch delivered by the cubic yard is often 50% cheaper than bagged.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many bags of mulch per cubic yard?
One cubic yard equals 27 cubic feet. Standard 2 cubic foot bags means you need approximately 13–14 bags per cubic yard.
How deep should mulch be?
3 inches is ideal for most garden beds — deep enough to suppress weeds but not so deep it starves roots of oxygen. Never pile mulch against plant stems or tree trunks.
How often should I replace mulch?
Organic mulch decomposes over 1–2 years. Add 1–2 inches each spring rather than replacing entirely — decomposed mulch improves soil quality.
